Guide to catching and befriending a Phrog.

Introduction

Phrogs are scaredy little creatures, and will avoid being caught in the wild, as they are known to enjoy their freedom very much...

However, once you earn their trust, they are very loyal friends to have. A little bit of patience and a lot of love (as well as some bait) is all you need to catch a phrog. So, let's get started !

Step 2: Luring the Phrog

Sometimes, Phrogs won't be lured by the smell of food alone. If you are even unable to find one in the wild, two things can help you:

Change the search location:
Indeed some locations are completely avoided by Phrogs. For example, they have a preference for wet or damp habitats, like rivers and ponds, because one of their favourite activities is swimming. Phrogs are very social animals, and they will respond to the call of another Phrog. If you are able to mimic the sound of a Phrog, you might be able to attract one to you.
